Alert

An alert is a warning or message about any context that can be closed by clicking a button. On a single page, there can be multiple alerts, each providing important information or notifications. These alerts can be used to inform users about various events, such as errors, updates, or confirmations. They help ensure that users are aware of critical information without overwhelming them. Additionally, alerts can be styled differently to indicate their level of importance, such as using colors or icons to distinguish between warnings, errors, and informational messages.

Structure

<div data-tg="alert">
  <p>Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, ct enim ipsa, quia ex hic cumque, ullam totam. Doloribus, cum!</p>
  <button>close</button>
</div>
