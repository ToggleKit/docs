Tab

Tabs are used to organize content into categories for easy access. When one category is visible, the others are hidden, allowing visitors to switch between them seamlessly. This design helps keep the interface clean and user-friendly, making it easier for users to find the information they need without being overwhelmed by too much content at once. Additionally, tabs can enhance the user experience by reducing the need for excessive scrolling and providing a more intuitive navigation system. By grouping related content together, tabs help users understand the structure of the information and quickly locate what theyâ€™re looking for. This approach is particularly useful for websites and applications with multiple sections or categories, ensuring a smooth and efficient browsing experience.
