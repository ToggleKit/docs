Text limit

Text limit slice the text how many word you want to show also add at the ellipsis . [data-textlimit=''] is expect a number value how many word need to show.

Structure

<p data-textlimit="10">Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipisicing elit. Nesciunt voluptatum eligendi fugit, voluptas, dolorem distinctio suscipit aut officia esse sapiente nulla quasi amet cupiditate. Sit corporis, ratione error nihil quisquam!</p>
